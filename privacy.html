Privacy Policy

Deals Content Manager — by Madonna Lily

Effective Date: March 3, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Deals Content Manager ("the App"), developed and operated by Madonna Lily ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Pinterest content management application. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

When you authorize the App through Pinterest, we may collect the following information:

  • Pinterest OAuth 2.0 Access Tokens — used to authenticate your account and perform actions on your behalf.
  • Board Information — names, descriptions, and identifiers of your Pinterest boards.
  • Pin Data — information related to pins created or managed through the App, including titles, descriptions, images, and destination links.
  • Basic Profile Information — your Pinterest username and account identifier, as provided by the Pinterest API.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • To create, schedule, and publish pins to your Pinterest boards only with your explicit approval.
  • To display your board information within the App so you can select where to post content.
  • To maintain your authenticated session so you do not need to re-authorize repeatedly.

We do not use your data for analytics, advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than providing the core functionality of the App.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your personal data or Pinterest information with any third parties. Your data is used exclusively to operate the App and is never disclosed to external services, advertisers, or data brokers.

5. Authentication & Password Security

The App uses Pinterest's OAuth 2.0 protocol for authentication. This means:

  • We never access, collect, or store your Pinterest password.
  • Authentication is handled entirely by Pinterest's secure authorization flow.
  • We only receive and store the OAuth access token provided by Pinterest after you grant permission.

6. Data Storage & Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. OAuth tokens are stored securely and are used only for authorized API requests. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Revoking Access

You may revoke the App's access to your Pinterest account at any time by visiting your Pinterest Account Settings and removing the App from your authorized applications. Once access is revoked, we will no longer be able to access your Pinterest data, and any stored tokens will become invalid.

8. Data Retention

We retain your OAuth tokens and associated data only for as long as necessary to provide the App's services. If you revoke access or request deletion of your data, we will remove your information from our systems in a timely manner.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
