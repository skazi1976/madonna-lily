Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 3, 2026

Introduction

Zara Vs Aliexpress ("we", "our", "us") operates a Pinterest integration application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you interact with our application.

Information We Collect

Our application accesses the following data through the Pinterest API:

  • Pinterest account information (username, account type)
  • Board information (board names and IDs)
  • Pin data (pins created through our application)

How We Use Information

We use the collected information solely for:

  • Creating and scheduling pins on your Pinterest boards
  • Managing your content publishing schedule

Data Storage

Authentication tokens are stored locally on your device. We do not store your data on external servers or share it with third parties.

Third-Party Services

Our application interacts with the Pinterest API. Your use of Pinterest is governed by Pinterest's own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information, including secure token storage and encrypted API communications.

Your Rights

You can revoke our application's access to your Pinterest account at any time through your Pinterest account settings.
